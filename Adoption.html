Adoption



Why Adopt?

Because of breed specific legislation, overbreeding, and prejudices, many Pitbulls end up at shelters. An effective way to combat this epidemic in the animal community would be to always look at adoption first. Many of these dogs which are housed in local shelters have passed behavioral tests and have already been properly medically cleared. The shelters also typically subsidize substantially or eliminate the cost of nueter/spaying procedures.
