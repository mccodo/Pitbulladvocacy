My Pit-Bull Advocacy

A Understanding of the Misunderstood Breed

Pit Bulls are often viewed as vicious dogs, who are inherently fighters and aggressive. This couldn’t be further from the truth, but many people are unable to bring their pit bulls when moving due to this image. While breed specific legislation is slowly starting to be overturned, apartment buildings and rental homes still deny “aggressive breeds.” Throughout this website, you will find resources to help change the image of pit bulls and also better support you when moving to new locations.
