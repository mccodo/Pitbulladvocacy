Information on Renting with Pittbulls




Some Additional Tips

  • Try to search for private listings. Due to insurance concerns, public housing has many breed restrictions.
  • Look for advocacy groups in your specific area to seek out affordable housing for you and your pup!
  • Use a broker to help find appropriate housing available in your area to lower your options to those that fit best.
  • Don't be afraid to negotiate. Many landlords will make exceptions based upon additional pet deposits, vet records, and obedience certifications.
